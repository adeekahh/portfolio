Ux Project
WAAAY

In this one month long assignment we had to come up with a digital concept to tackle a prolem we wanted to solve.

❮ ❯

The biggest and most complex assignment of the semester started with a group problem formulation. We used several brainstorming methods to choose one from our ideas. We had time to make plans for 4 weekly sprints that we did at the beggining of the process, then distributed the tasks on a kanban chart to make it easy to follow for everyone in the group.

The problem we finally chose is people geting bored of their everyday routine and feeling really connected to their surrounding.

After our research and target group and market analysis we differentiated two audiences in our target group, locals and travellers.

This was followed by the first steps of setting up a new company, describing its vision mission and values, filling out the relevant fields of a business model canvas and coming up with a selling proposition.

After organizing our findings in the report we started to work on the visual identity of the company aided by a Design brief and a a Design Program. This was followed by the actual design of the application on an aesthetic, functional and interaction level.

My role was to make a customer survey that serves with relevant information about the problems and needs of our potentional customers and based on the findings come up with basic personas.In the second part of the project I mostly worked on the aesthetics of the application to make it appealing for our users and to convey the essentional message of our company.
