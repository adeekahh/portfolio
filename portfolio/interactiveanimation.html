Interactive Animation

The task was to make an interactive animation based on a novel or movie in a given style of art.

❮ ❯

The style that we got with my team was punk. We wanted to target our product to a niche of the given target group of young adults interested in art. For this niche we chose young single girls and to be able to make our final product appealing to them we chose a movie that strongly contrasts with our given style. This is why we chose the movie: Crazy, Stupid Love.

We started the process with brainstorming about a storyboard for our animation, that led us to have an outline of what we want to achieve at the early stage of the process. To narrow down our ideas we used a moodboard and a personas collage that later helped us at creating the color scheme for our scene.

To make clear the responsibilities of each person in our group we divided the tasks for pairs, this way we could provide a more efficient workflow for our project. I was in the pair working on the graphics. I mostly worked on the zombies attacking the protagonists and also helped to match the characters to our given style with tattoos and additional garments.
