Redesigned Website

The second project was to redesign an existing website for a company called Gates And Fences.

❮ ❯

During the redesign assignment each group was assigned to make over an existing website for a company. My team got the task to redesign the website of a company selling gates and fences.

The most obvious problem with the site was the information overload, lack of implementation of design principles and the unclear way of navigation. The 5 second tests we did on our fellow classmates also proved this.

We started redesign process with a clear moodboard that helped the whole team to keep an aim at the end goal. After that we designed a style tile, then based on the decisions made to create that we designed our first version of prototypes, what we tested with expert tests and implemented the findings into our next version. We repeated this process until we got a satisfactory feedback.

The biggest challange we faced was to apply the principles of professional groupwork to our process because it was the first assigmnent in the semester that had to be solved as a group which took time for us to adjust.
