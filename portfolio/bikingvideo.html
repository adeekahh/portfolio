Prompting an Idea
Biking Video

The second assignment in the theme was to make a video popularizing getting around Copenhagen by bike for newcomers.

After forming groups we made a short treatment about the video we were going to make and started to work on the storyboard for our project with dramaturgic elements and the retorics of the voice-over in mind.

At a point in the process we had to make changes because of results of our research and come up with a new concept and restart the process in a faster pace.

The last step of the assignment was to present the video as an embedded element on a HTML5 on a website.

My part mostly consisted of editing the video as well as helping with the shooting and developing the concept.
