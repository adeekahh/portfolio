Story Without Words
Short Video

In this 3-day assignment every group had to make a short video with phone based on one of the three topics:
- Urban Living
- Pople who are too busy
- Difficult liasions

With my team we chose the first given theme called, Urban living.

Our goal was to capture the monotony of an everyday routine by making the user feel what a person repeating a daily cycle of seeing the same things, faces, places would feel. At the end of the video we wanted to represent a break from this with showing the protagonist escaping to the nature.

For the realization of the video we considered basic composition, lightings, audio recording and editing techniques.
